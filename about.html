Our Story

Where every dessert starts with a real reason

🏆 Clifton's #1 Bakery — BusinessRate 2026

In June 2026, BusinessRate ranked D'era House the #1 bakery in Clifton, NJ — first place in every category. This recognition belongs to our customers: every review, every returning face, every recommendation to a friend made it possible. Thank you, Clifton. See the full rankings.

How It Started

D'era House didn't start as a business plan. It started as a kitchen obsession, a need to make things by hand, the right way, and share them with people who could taste the difference. What began as baking for family and friends grew into something the whole neighborhood started talking about. Now we're here, at 1076 Main Ave in Clifton, doing what we love every single day.

Woman-Owned, Community-Rooted

D'era House is proudly woman-owned and operated. We believe a great bakery isn't measured by how many locations it has, it's measured by whether the person behind the counter actually cares. We do. Every order matters to us, whether it's a single cheesecake slice or a three-tier wedding cake.

Why We Do It This Way

We don't use frozen dough or pre-made fillings. We don't cut corners on ingredients. Everything you eat from D'era House was made today, because that's the only way we know how to do it. The baklava recipe hasn't changed. The cheesecake base hasn't changed. When something works, you don't mess with it.

What Our Customers Mean to Us

"This bakery is more than a business, it's the place we built so that every person who walks through the door feels like they're getting something made just for them. That's the goal every morning when we start baking."
